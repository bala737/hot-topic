The Value of Money

Often times, people say that they can live without money. They define money as just one of the tools that enhances peoples living environment. However, in real life money is a very important matter in peoples lives. Although the people in history might have lived through the exchange of goods and not relying to the value of money itself, modern society today could not function without money. Money plays a huge role in the society in variety of ways such as in business, at peoples job, and even in education. Money helps people achieve a better quality of education, larger chance of business success, and higher work output.
